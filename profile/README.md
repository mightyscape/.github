## Core Product Areas

### 1. IPTV & OTT Platform Stack

A modular, production-grade platform that supports:
- Live TV, VOD, Catch-up TV
- Android TV, Mobile, Smart TV ecosystems
- Multi-tenant operator setups (LCO / MSO models)
- Scalable backend services and APIs

Designed to grow from **hundreds to millions of users** without re-architecture.

---

### 2. Content Security & DRM Systems

Content protection is essential—but often unaffordable.

Our products focus on:
- ClearKey-based DRM for cost-sensitive platforms
- Widevine-ready architectures
- License servers, key management, and policy enforcement
- Secure playback without unnecessary overhead

Built with **unit economics** in mind, not just compliance checklists.

---

### 3. Streaming & Media Delivery Infrastructure

We build products around:
- Efficient live and VOD streaming workflows
- ABR-ready pipelines
- Origin and edge delivery integration
- Intelligent failover and health-check mechanisms

Optimized for **real-world networks**, not ideal lab conditions.

---

### 4. Operator & Admin Platforms

A unified control layer that enables:
- Subscriber management
- Content control
- License monitoring
- Usage analytics
- Operational visibility

Designed for **operators, not engineers**.

## What Problems Our Products Solve

### High Cost of Running Entertainment Platforms
Many platforms fail not because of lack of users—but because of:
- DRM cost per subscriber
- Infrastructure overprovisioning
- Third-party dependency overload

Our products are built to **reduce recurring operational costs** without compromising reliability.

---

### Fragmented Entertainment Ecosystems
Operators often manage:
- Multiple vendors
- Incompatible systems
- Limited visibility

We focus on **consolidation and simplicity**—one platform, one control layer.

---

### Lack of Flexibility for Growing Platforms
Most solutions lock platforms into:
- Fixed pricing models
- Rigid architectures
- Limited customization

Our products are **modular and extensible**, allowing platforms to evolve as business needs change.
